--- layout: page title: "Privacy Policy for Trident" date: 2015-08-24 11:27:21 ---

We don't store any personal information about you. We do need to log some things though to help improve Trident.

This privacy document has recently been updated with Trident version 1.5.

Trident uses Twitter Fabric.io (Answers) to send analytics about how you generally use the App. We send events such as when you view your Pull Requests, create an Issue, determine if you're using GitHub or GitLab, and many other events. We don't send sensitive information about you or your projects contents in these activity events and we absolutely respect your privacy. We use these events to better understand what features are being used in Trident.

If you opt in to sending crash reports, these will be sent directly to Twitter Crashlytics. Crash report results will only ever be used to fix issues with Trident.

If you signup for E-mail updates about Trident, your E-mail address will be stored with MailChimp until you choose to unsubscribe. We will never deliberately share your E-mail address to anyone.

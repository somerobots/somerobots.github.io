--- layout: page title: "Privacy Policy for Trident" date: 2015-08-24 11:27:21 ---

We don't want to store any information about you or how you use Trident. We do need some things though to help improve Trident.

If you opt in to sending crash reports, these will be sent directly to Apple. Crash report results will only ever be used to fix issues with Trident.

If you signup for E-mail updates about Trident, your E-mail address will be stored with MailChimp until you choose to unsubscribe. We will never deliberately share your E-mail address to anyone.
